# ⏱️ Lapse - Track your app install dates easily

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Lapse-blue)](https://github.com/alexialv5477/Lapse) 

Lapse helps you monitor your Android applications. It shows the exact date you installed or updated each app on your device. The tool runs completely offline. It protects your privacy by keeping your data on your phone.

## 📋 Features

* **See install dates**: Find out exactly when you installed your apps.
* **Track updates**: See when you last updated each piece of software.
* **Offline mode**: No data leaves your device.
* **Clean design**: The app uses modern design standards for simple navigation.
* **Lightweight**: The tool runs fast without draining your battery.
* **No ads**: You get a distraction-free experience.
* **No trackers**: Your habits stay private.

## ⚙️ Requirements

* An Android device running version 10.0 or higher.
* Approximately 10 MB of free storage space.
* Permission to read installed package information on your device.

## 🚀 How to use Lapse

Once you open the app, it scans your phone automatically. You will see a list of all your installed software. Each entry shows the app name, the original installation date, and the most recent update date. You can scroll through this list to browse your apps. You can also use the search bar to find specific programs.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

Lapse processes all data locally. This means your app history never reaches the internet. The app does not request external network permissions. Your privacy remains your own. You control what information stays on your device.

## 🛠️ Frequently Asked Questions

**Why does the app need permissions?**
Lapse needs access to your list of installed apps to show their timestamps. It uses this read-only access to generate your report.

**Can I export my data?**
The app focuses on showing you the data directly on your screen. 

**Will this app work on older Android phones?**
This tool requires Android 10.0 or newer. It does not support older versions of the operating system.

**Does this app track my usage patterns?**
No. The app does not collect information about how you use other software. It only reads the system metadata regarding install and update dates.

## 🧩 Understanding timestamps

The install date reflects when the package first arrived on your device. The update date shows when you applied the latest version from your app store or manual installation. These dates help you identify how long you have kept an app or when a bug might have started after an update.

## 🎯 Troubleshooting issues

If the app fails to open, check your available storage space. If the app does not list all your programs, ensure you granted the required read permissions in your phone settings. Restart your device if the list does not refresh after an update.

## 📁 Project Details

Lapse utilizes Java to communicate with the Android package manager. The interface follows the Material Design 3 guidelines for a smooth look. The small size ensures that it does not impact your phone performance.
